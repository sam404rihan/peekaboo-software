② POS Terminal — Making a Sale

Click the red New Sale button in the sidebar (always visible) or navigate to POS. This is the main billing screen.

Checkout Flow
  1. Scan or search — use a barcode scanner or type the product name/barcode. Press Enter to add the first result instantly.
  2. Adjust quantity using the + / − buttons in the cart.
  3. Select a customer — type their name or phone. New customer? Enter details and they are saved automatically.
  4. Apply a coupon (optional) — enter the code in the coupon field. Automatic offers apply without any code.
  5. Choose payment method — Cash, Card, UPI, or a split across methods.
  6. Click Checkout — the invoice is created and the receipt appears immediately.
Offline mode: If the internet drops the POS keeps working normally. Sales queue in local storage and sync automatically when connectivity returns. An Offline badge appears at the top when this is active.

③ Invoices

Every completed sale lives here. Go to Invoices in the sidebar.

View & Search

Filter by invoice number, customer name, or date range. Click any row to see the full breakdown — items, GST split, payment method.

Print Receipt

Open an invoice → click the Print icon. A print-ready receipt page opens. You can also copy the receipt link to share with the customer.

Exchange / Return

Open an invoice → click Exchange. Select which items to return and whether they go back to sellable stock or are marked as damaged.

Add New Items

During an exchange you can also add new products. The system creates a new invoice for the difference and updates stock in one step.

④ Customers

Go to Customers to see everyone who has ever purchased.

  • Search by name or phone number.
  • Click a customer to see their full purchase history and current loyalty points balance.
  • Loyalty points are earned automatically at checkout and can be redeemed on the next sale.

⑤ Inventory Admin

Go to Inventory to manage your product catalog and stock.

Adding a New Product

  1. Click New Product (top-right).
  2. Fill in Name, Category, Barcode (auto-generated if left blank), MRP (selling price), Rate (purchase/cost price), and starting Stock.
  3. Set Tax Rate — usually 18% GST. For products with variable GST, enter a Threshold Price (see below).
  4. Click Save.
Dynamic GST (Threshold Price): If a product has a Threshold Price set, the tax rate switches automatically at billing — selling price below threshold → 5% GST; at or above → 18% GST. Useful for items like footwear regulated under slab-based GST.

Restocking — Receiving New Stock

Go to Settings → Receive Stock.

  1. Search for the product.
  2. Enter the quantity received and the purchase rate.
  3. Submit — stock is updated and an Inventory Log entry is written automatically.

⑦ Offers & Coupons Admin

Discount Decision Tree

Offers — Settings → Offers

Flat

Fixed rupee discount — e.g. ₹50 off every purchase.

Percentage

e.g. 10% off the cart total.

BOGO

Buy One Get One free — applies to the same item only.

Birthday (DOB)

Targets customers whose birthday month matches the current month.

If multiple offers match, set a Priority number — the highest priority offer wins.

Coupons — Settings → Coupons

Create a code, set an expiry date, a usage limit (or unlimited), and a discount value (flat or %). Customers enter the code at the POS coupon field.

⑨ How Taxes Work

All prices are MRP-inclusive — the tax is already baked into the selling price. Nothing extra is added on top at checkout.
Tax Breakdown on a Receipt

Discounts are applied post-tax and do not reduce the GST figure — the GST is always calculated on the undiscounted MRP.

⑩ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the POS without internet?
Yes. Bill normally — sales queue locally and sync when you come back online. Look for the Offline badge.
Where do I update the store name / GSTIN on receipts?
Settings → Business Profile.
How do I process a return or refund?
Open the original invoice → click Exchange → select items to return → choose sellable (goes back to stock) or damaged (written off).
A sale is stuck in the offline queue — what do I do?
Confirm your internet is active. If the issue persists, go to Settings → Offline Queue to inspect or clear the stuck operation.
How do I print barcode labels for new stock?
Settings → Barcodes → search product → choose label count → print. Labels are 50×25 mm, Code-128 format.
Who can create new users / change roles?
User creation is done via the admin debug panel or Firebase console. Contact your system administrator.
